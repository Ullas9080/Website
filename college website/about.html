About the BCA Department

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in computer education and research, fostering innovation and creating leaders in the IT industry.

Our Mission

  • To provide high-quality education and practical training.
  • To promote research and innovation in computer applications.
  • To collaborate with industry for the holistic development of students.
  • To inculcate ethical values and a sense of responsibility in our students.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
  • Certifications in specialized areas such as Data Science, AI, Cybersecurity, etc.
